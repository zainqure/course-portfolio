My Top 5 Favorite Anime

  1. The Dangers in My Heart

    A romantic and funny story about a shy boy who slowly opens up after meeting a kind and popular girl.

  2. The Fragrant Flower Blooms with Dignity

    A sweet and touching love story between two students from different school who grow closer over time.

  3. Lookism

    A boy wakes up with a different handsome bodies learns about beauty, kindness, and real strength.

  4. The Eminence in Shadow

    A boy who dreams of being a secret hero builds a hidden organization, not knowing his fake story becomes real.

  5. Tokyo Revengers

    A man travels back in time to fix his past and save the people he cares about, mixing action, friendship, and emotion.
