My Favorite Anime & Manga Collection

This is my personal collection of anime and manga that I love. Each series shown here brings me joy, whether it's because it's funny, emotional, or full of exciting adventures. I enjoy watching and reading these stories and they inspire me in different ways.

The Dangers in My Heart

Anime | Romance, Comedy | 2023

A funny and romantic story about a shy boy who slowly opens up after meeting a kind and popular girl. I enjoy it because it's heartwarming and makes me laugh.

The Fragrant Flower Blooms with Dignity

Anime | Romance, School Life | 2022

A sweet love story between two students from different schools who grow closer over time. It's touching and makes me feel happy when I watch it.

Lookism

Anime | Drama, Action | 2022

A boy wakes up in a different, handsome body and learns about beauty, kindness, and real strength. I like how it teaches lessons about self-worth and empathy.

The Eminence in Shadow

Anime | Action, Fantasy | 2022

A boy dreams of being a secret hero and builds a hidden organization, not knowing his fake story becomes real. I enjoy the suspense and clever humor.

Tokyo Revengers

Anime | Action, Time Travel, Drama | 2021

A man travels back in time to fix his past and save people he cares about. I love the mix of action, friendship, and emotions in the story.

Romantic Killer

Anime | Romance, Comedy | 2022

A fun and romantic story about a girl whose life is turned upside down by a mysterious little monster. I enjoy it because it's funny, cute, and full of unexpected twists in the love story.
